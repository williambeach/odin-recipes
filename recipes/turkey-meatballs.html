Turkey Meatballs

To Get Started, You Need Two Things:

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs Ground Turkey
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 2/3 cup minced onion
  • 1/2 cup fresh minced parsley
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cloves minced garlic
  • 2 tsps Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 1/4 cup olive oil

A Creative Touch:

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Line a rimmed baking sheet with foil for easy cleanup. Coat a wire rack with nonstick spray and set on prepared baking sheet.
  2. Arrange meatballs on rack, brush with oil, and bake until browned with crispy edges, about 15 to 20 minutes (an internal thermometer should read 165 degrees for 15 seconds).
